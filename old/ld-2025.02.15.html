Law and democracy

As a public interest lawyer, my expertise includes the U.S. Constitution, strategic democracy litigation, campaign finance, voting rights, and environmental law.

I'm Chief Counsel at American Oversight, where our team of dedicated lawyers fights for truth, accountability, and democracy by enforcing the public's right to government records. Previously, I served for a decade as the Legal Director at Free Speech For People, and before that at the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.

If you want to contact me about something American Oversight-related, please visit the American Oversight web site.

Cases

Growe v. Simon - candidate eligibility challenge to Donald Trump under the Fourteenth Amendment’s Insurrectionist Disqualification Clause

In re challenge to constitutional qualifications of Representative Marjorie Taylor Greene - candidate eligibility challenge under the Fourteenth Amendment’s Insurrectionist Disqualification Clause (first case to compel a U.S. Representative to testify under oath about their involvement in January 6, 2021)

In re challenge to constitutional qualifications of Representative Madison Cawthorn - candidate eligibility challenge under the Fourteenth Amendment’s Insurrectionist Disqualification Clause (first such challenge since Reconstruction)

Lieu v. Federal Election Commission - constitutional challenge to the lower court decision that created super PACs (with Stanford Supreme Court Litigation Clinic, Laurence Tribe, Albert Alschuler, Richard Painter, and others) (read the petition for certiorari)

CAIR-Minnesota v. Atlas Aegis - challenge to private voter intimidation under the Voting Rights Act of 1965 that was only the third case in the act’s history to yield injunctive relief for the plaintiffs, and the first in the act’s history to result in a post-election consent decree following a pre-election injunction

Mi Familia Vota v. Trump - challenge to voter intimidation by the president of the United States under the Voting Rights Act of 1965

Amicus brief in Williams-Yulee v. Florida Bar (constitutional case involving anti-solicitation rule for judicial candidates) (2014)

Amicus brief in Conestoga Wood Specialties v. Sebelius (constitutional companion case to Hobby Lobby) (2014)
